**Constraints:**

- 1 <= nums.length <= 105
- -231 <= nums[i] <= 231 - 1

### Approach

- uses **cyclic sort** to place elements in its correcct place.
- It tries to place every nums[i] in i-1, i.e., in the home indices of every number,, i.e., 0->1,1->2 etc
- This solves the problem in O(1) space and O(n) time.
