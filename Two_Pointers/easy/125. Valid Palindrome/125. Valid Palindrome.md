**Constraints:**

- 1 <= s.length <= 2 * 105
- s consists only of printable ASCII characters.


### Approach:

- We have to consider both letters and digits.
- When parsing from beginning to the end, we have to skip elements if they are neither letters nor digits.
- After skipping those symbols and spaces, compare the characters at the start and end, and return true or false based on that.
